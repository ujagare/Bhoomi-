Clear answers for clients planning rockfall protection, guniting, slope stabilization and civil construction work.

We provide guniting, bungalow construction, retaining walls, anchoring services, rockfall protection, slope stabilization and related project execution support.

02 Do you work on highways, railways and tunnel projects?

Yes, our solutions are suitable for roads, railways, tunnels, mining areas, hydropower sites and infrastructure projects with challenging terrain conditions.

Yes, every site is assessed for terrain, risk, access and project requirements before recommending the right technical approach and execution plan.
