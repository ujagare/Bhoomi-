Who We Are

Bhoomi Constructions And Rockfall Protection

Bhoomi Constructions and Rockfall Protection is a trusted name in specialized civil engineering and construction, providing comprehensive solutions in rockfall related locations for railways, highways and infrastructure.

With proven technical expertise and field experience, we offer services including guniting services, bungalow construction, retaining walls, anchoring services, geotechnical solutions, rockfall and end-to-end project execution.

We specialize in guniting services for critical applications such as slope, tunnel, rock walls, bridge underpasses and advanced spraying techniques to ensure precision, durability and minimal downtime.
